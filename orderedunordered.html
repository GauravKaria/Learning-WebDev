Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For Dough

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk

for filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add in flour
  4. Knead for 10 minutes
  5. transfer into a large bowl
